Interactive Map

Follow the line through the city

The map now uses current RTA GTFS stop coordinates for the St. Charles timing-point pins. These are exact stop points aligned to the published schedule checkpoints, not every physical stop pole on the full line. It also highlights the current Route 11 (Magazine) corridor along the Magazine segment that is most relevant to this guide, featured stops are color-coded by type, and the marker menu lets you show one marker type at a time or keep all markers visible.
